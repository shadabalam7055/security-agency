We Are Looking For

  • Security Guards: Vigilant physical safeguarding for corporate and retail structures.

Required Documents

Applicants must possess clear copies of the following documents during the screening process:

  • Aadhaar Card (National ID)

  • Educational Certificates (Minimum High School equivalent)
  • Address Proof (Utility bill / rental agreement)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph (White background)
